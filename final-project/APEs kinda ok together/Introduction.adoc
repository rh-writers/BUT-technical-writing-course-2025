##  Prerequisites

* a laptop or a computer
* a working pair of hands
* a working brain 
* logical thinking
* working eyes (at least one)


## Procedure

To become a great technical writer, you need to follow certain guidelines.

### What you should aim for:

* accessibility
* readability
* findability
* minimalism
